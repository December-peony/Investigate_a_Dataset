Introduction¶

Dataset Description¶

  • This dataset have 14 features.
  • Most of them are selfdescribing.
  • Handcap(Not binary) represent the number of disabilites the patient have. - https://www.kaggle.com/joniarroba/noshowappointments/discussion/29699#229356 -
  • SMS(binary) indicates if patient recevied SMS as a reminder.

Questions for Analysis:¶

At first we think about our target and try to assume how feature affect the target.

  • People may forget their appointments, so first question, Does the SMS number affect the target?
  • Many things can happen in a span of a month or week, so, Does the difference between reservation date and actual date affect the target?
  • Does the type of disease affect the target?

Finally,

Conclusions:¶

  • The number of waiting days affect the no_show, the less the day the higher the chance that the pateient will come.
  • Receving a message makes a higher chance that the patient will come to the appointement.
  • People with Hypertension is the least likely to come and people with diabetes is the most likely to come.
  • Scholarship may or may not affect the no show, needs more information.

Limitations:¶

  • Varibles are not very clear and the dataset creator itself is inconsistent in some things. ex: Handicap and SMS.
  • Categorical data limitations. Needs more of them and in another arrange.
